Welcome to TripSync!

Plan Your Perfect Trip in Any City with Fun Activities!

TripSync is a revolutionary web app designed to help you plan your trips with ease, syncing weather forecasts and activities based on your location.

Project Features

  • Weather forecast view of each day in the itinerary.
  • User profile system, and can add other users to trips.
