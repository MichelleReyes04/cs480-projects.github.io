About Kamino

Kamino is a web-based administration panel for the CPP Student Data Center. It is designed to streamline the management of student data and improve the overall user experience.

Visit Kamino

To connect to the Kamino website, you must have valid credentials for the Global Protect VPN (use vpn.sdc.cpp.edu as the portal)
