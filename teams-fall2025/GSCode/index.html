Our Project

Our project is a web interface for a new and improved version of CPP MyBar,
a platform for students to view and filter campus clubs.

  • Tag-based searching and more advanced filters
  • Club profiles automatically display latest social media posts and disregards needs of manual updates
  • Better user experience for both members and officers
