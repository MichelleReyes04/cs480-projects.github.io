2A2E - Sec+ Study Tool

Course: CS 4800 – Software Engineering, Fall 2025

About Our Project

Our project is a lightweight study tool for students preparing for the CompTIA Security+ certification. It focuses on:

  • Flashcards by Security+ domain
  • Quick multiple-choice quizzes
  • Progress tracking (future feature)

Team Members

  • Adam Cheung – Product & Database
  • Chenrui Zhang – backend logic
  • Edmund Cheung – UI/UX
  • Aaron Lo - Frontend

Goals

Our goal is to help students review effectively with short, daily sessions and clear progress feedback. The tool will be completely browser-based and easy to use.
